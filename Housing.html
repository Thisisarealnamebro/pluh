JVC

Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC) is a popular community known for its affordable living and family-friendly atmosphere. It features a mix of villas and apartments, with many parks, schools, and retail spaces. The community is still developing, making it an attractive option for those looking for newer, more spacious homes with easy access to key areas of Dubai.

Rental Prices: Villas: AED 90,000 - AED 150,000/year; Apartments: AED 35,000 - AED 80,000/year.

Sale Prices: Villas: AED 1.5 million - AED 3 million; Apartments: AED 400,000 - AED 1.5 million.

Emirates Hills

Emirates Hills is one of Dubai’s most exclusive and luxurious communities. This gated community is home to expansive villas set along a beautiful golf course. It’s known for its tranquil environment, security, and proximity to top schools and the Dubai Marina, offering a serene escape while still being close to the city.

Rental Prices: Villas: AED 600,000 - AED 3 million/year.

Sale Prices: Villas: AED 20 million - AED 100 million.

The Springs

The Springs is a highly sought-after, family-oriented community in Dubai, offering affordable townhouses surrounded by lakes and lush greenery. It’s perfect for those who want a quiet, suburban lifestyle with access to outdoor activities, while still being close to Dubai’s major roads and attractions.

Rental Prices: Villas: AED 90,000 - AED 200,000/year.

Sale Prices: Villas: AED 1.5 million - AED 4 million.

JBR

Jumeirah Beach Residence (JBR) offers a lively, beachfront lifestyle. It features spacious apartments and an abundance of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Residents enjoy walking along the beachfront promenade, enjoying watersports, and having easy access to nearby attractions like the Dubai Marina and Palm Jumeirah.

Rental Prices: Villas: Starting at AED 300,000/year (limited availability); Apartments: AED 75,000 - AED 250,000/year.

Sale Prices: Villas: Starting at AED 5 million (limited availability); Apartments: AED 1.5 million - AED 8 million.

Arabian Ranches

Arabian Ranches is a premium, family-friendly community that offers spacious villas set amidst landscaped gardens. The community is quiet and secure, with access to parks, golf courses, and schools. It’s perfect for those looking for a more suburban lifestyle while still being within reach of the city center and major roads.

Rental Prices: Villas: AED 160,000 - AED 400,000/year.

Sale Prices: Villas: AED 2 million - AED 15 million.
