Dubai College

Dubai College is a prestigious British curriculum school, known for academic excellence and extensive extracurricular offerings. It prepares students for top universities worldwide, with a strong focus on sciences, humanities, and the arts.

Jumeirah College

Jumeirah College follows the British curriculum and is highly regarded for its academic rigor and diverse extracurricular programs. It focuses on nurturing well-rounded students prepared for top universities.

Dubai International Academy (DIA)

Dubai International Academy, located in Emirates Hills, offers the International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum and is known for its academic excellence and global focus. With a diverse student body, DIA provides a variety of extracurriculars including sports, arts, and tech clubs.

GEMS Modern Academy (GMA)

GEMS Modern Academy offers the Indian ICSE curriculum through primary and middle school, with an IB Diploma option for senior students. Known for a balanced focus on academics and personal growth, GMA provides robust facilities for sports, arts, and music.

Kings' School Dubai

Kings' School Dubai is a sought-after British curriculum school known for its nurturing environment and commitment to high academic standards. The school emphasizes creativity, innovation, and individual talent development.
